About Us

North Karnataka is scorching hot. Chilled beverages which kill the heat have been a part of the region’s culture for centuries, with each household having their own treasured recipes which have been passed down for generations. Kemps has an archive of the best recipes that the North has to offer. The rich cultural heritage being imbibed into the products that kemp’s offer is a proof of it being one of the oldest food and beverage companies. It has been one of the firsts in India to receive ISI certification. It has the one of the largest beverage manufacturing facility in Karnataka, Equipped with the latest beverage technology, Kemp’s has one of the largest beverage manufacturing facility in Karnataka that is centered around organic production methods. Kemp’s is known for its innovativeness and novelty in products as a result of their constant research efforts to produce what people love best!
