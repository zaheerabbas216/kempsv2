About Kemp's

Every drop of Kemp's Mineral Water is safe, pure, and sanitary to the core and complies with the quality standards established by the Bureau of Indian Standards, making it stand out for its promise of goodness (BIS).

Kemp's Mineral Water is available in a variety of sizes for every individual thirsts as well as sharing with family and friends.

Kemp's has been a well-known brand for many years and is a representation of goodness, trust, and purity. It is the most reputable brand of mineral water in Karnataka and a pioneer in its industry. Kemp's has a significant presence and has kept its commitment to customers for the past 25 years to provide safe, pure, and healthy mineral water.

While producing our products, we comply with strict quality code that meets international requirements. To guarantee that our customers always receive pure, safe, and nutritious drinking water.
