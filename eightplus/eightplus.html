Why choose Alkaline water?

The pH level of alkaline water is greater than that of ordinary drinking water. Because of this, some proponents of alkaline water assert that it helps balance your body's acid levels. The pH of typical drinking water is typically 7. A pH of 8 or 9 is common for alkaline water. However, pH by alone is insufficient to significantly increase the alkalinity of water. Alkaline minerals and water with a low oxidation-reduction potential are also necessary (ORP). Water's ORP refers to its capacity to function as a pro- or antioxidant. The more anti-oxidizing something is, the more negative its ORP value is.

The pH value ranges from 0-14:

  • Acidic: 0.0-6.9
  • Neutral: 7.0
  • Alkaline (or basic): 7.1-14.0
